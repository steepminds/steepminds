The Audi Autonomous driving cup took place in May 2016. More than 10 universities participated in the competition where our team was representing Univeristy of Freiburg. For the main event, the teams were given tasks to use data science and machine learning technologies for cars to avoid obstacles, park themselves and perform one special task with the team's own idea. Amanullah Tariq was a member of the team responsible for creating the machine learning algorithms used behind the obstacles avoiding technology in the cars. The event turned out to be a success for University of Freiburg.
