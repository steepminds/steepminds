SM Butler

SM Butler is a highly flexible point of sale system created by steepminds in 2015. The product is currently used by more than 10 customers across Germany. The modular software is designed to cater different market sizes ranging from large scale companies to small shops. The flexibility to use as many modules as you need gives you the opportunity to start your business with low investment and high support in your daily business activites. Currently the following modules are availble in the product.

  • Price lookup
  • Transaction and item discounts
  • Void transaction
  • Special orders
  • Time-limited specials
  • Items on hold
  • Revenue totals
  • Multi-system integration
  • Cash register
  • Barcode scanner
  • Credit card scanner
  • Receipt/label printer
  • Wireless support
  • Security levels
  • Credit card scanner
